# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

Lliam-GOV is a small, governed project. Everyone who interacts with it — maintainers, authorized contributors, and anyone filing an issue — is expected to help keep the space respectful, professional, and harassment-free, regardless of background or identity.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and constructive. Critique ideas, not people.
- Assume good faith and keep discussion on-topic.
- Respect the project's governed nature: follow the contribution and security processes in [`CONTRIBUTING.md`](CONTRIBUTING.md) and [`SECURITY.md`](SECURITY.md).

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks.
- Publishing others' private information without consent.
- Deliberately disruptive, off-topic, or bad-faith conduct.

## Scope

This applies in all project spaces — issues, pull requests, and any official communication channel — and when representing the project in public.

## Enforcement

Report concerns privately to the maintainer (see the contact in [`SECURITY.md`](SECURITY.md); maintainer to confirm the real address). Reports will be reviewed and handled discreetly. Maintainers may remove content, or restrict participation, for behavior they judge inappropriate.

---

This Code of Conduct is adapted in spirit from the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/), v2.1.
